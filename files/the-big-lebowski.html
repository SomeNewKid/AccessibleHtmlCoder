The Big Lebowski

"The Big Lebowski" is a quirky, hilarious ride through the world of bowling, mistaken identity, and a rug that really ties the room together. Jeff Bridges shines as "The Dude," a laid-back slacker who gets caught up in a bizarre kidnapping plot. With its eccentric characters and memorable quotes, this cult classic is a must-watch for anyone who enjoys offbeat humor.
